Guaranteed Asset Protection (GAP) insurance covers the difference between what you owe on your loan and what your vehicle is worth if it's totaled or stolen. Available for just $399 — a one-time fee added to your loan balance. Dealerships typically charge $700–$1,200 for the same coverage.
