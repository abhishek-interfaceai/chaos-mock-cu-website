Checking & Savings:

Certificate of deposit products are available as Traditional IRAs, Roth IRAs, Coverdell ESAs and HSAs. Dividends are paid from current income and available earnings after required transfers to reserves. Certificates of deposit and Flex Certificates of Deposit are available as Traditional and Roth or Educational Savings Certificates.

***Business Analysis Checking features an Earnings Credit rate of 0.80%. Earnings credit calculates the average daily balance of the Analysis Checking and applies a designated earnings rate monthly used to offset Account Maintenance Fee, Per Item Fees and Miscellaneous Fees. Any additional earnings above the fee(s) offset will not be carried forward or credited to the Members account.

Rates displayed for Free Student Checking are also the rates for Free College Checking.

Individual Retirement Account (IRA) Annual contribution limits apply and penalties may apply for early withdrawal. Penalties could reduce your earnings. Please consult your tax advisor for additional information.

Dividends are paid from current income and available earnings after required transfers to reserves. Fees may reduce earnings. Certificate of Deposit Minimum Deposit Requirements: $25 for a My Savings Starter Certificate of Deposit or a 6-month term My Savings Builder Certificate of Deposit, $500 for regular Certificates of Deposit, and $1,000 for Flex Certificates of Deposit. The My Savings Builder Certificate of Deposit has a $1,000 deposit maximum and cannot be opened as an IRA or ESA. Certificates of Deposit and IRA Certificates may have a penalty for early withdrawal.

Rates and payments may increase after the initial fixed period.
